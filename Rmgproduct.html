Our products

As the industry becomes increasingly saturated, we focus on providing high quality products in well diverse fabrication to differentiate us from our competitors. We provide a wide range of Knit Apparels products including the following: Polo Shirts, T-Shirts, Tank Tops, Trousers, Hooded Jacket & Cardigans, Athleisure Wear, lounge Wear, Men’s & Ladies Fashions Wear, Kids Wear, etc....
We have established highly skilled and efficient quality control and production teams in all our factories. Our team’s collaborative effort ensures that we archive the high-quality standards of our customers and deliver the products on-time. Panam Group supplies high-quality apparels to globally renowned retailers and is focused on further enhancing the reputation of the readymade garments and textiles industry of Bangladesh.
