DYED YARN

Panam Group has launched Yarn dyeing to deliver the best quality clothing to our valuable customers in the fastest possible time. Which is equipped with the most advanced and latest technology machines in the present world. We have well experienced, technically sound skilled staffs to run this unit. For yarn dyeing process, Panam Group uses state-of-the-art machinery of SSM Brand from Switzerland, SMEW from India, GANVANINE from Germany and STRAYFIELD from England.
